Project Goals & Objectives

  • Secure Prescription Management: Create an immutable blockchain-based system for storing and verifying prescription data, preventing tampering and fraud.
  • Anti-Counterfeit Medicine Tracking: Implement a comprehensive batch tracking system to verify medicine authenticity and prevent fake drugs from entering the supply chain.
  • Multi-Stakeholder Platform: Develop separate portals for doctors, patients, and pharmacies with role-based access control and secure authentication.
  • QR Code Integration: Enable instant prescription verification through QR code scanning, allowing pharmacies to verify prescriptions in real-time.
  • Transparent Supply Chain: Track medicine batches from manufacturer to patient, ensuring complete traceability and accountability.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Provide comprehensive analytics dashboard for monitoring prescriptions, medicine batches, and system usage.
